Place of Origin: Billings, MT

Billings, Montana made headlines in 2019 when geologists working the sandstone cliffs along the Yellowstone River uncovered what appeared to be the most sophisticated artificial organism ever discovered. The metallic endoskeleton, dubbed "Chronos-1" by researchers, displayed an intricate fusion of organic and synthetic materials unlike anything in the archaeological record. Carbon dating of the surrounding sediment layers suggested the specimen was impossibly ancient, yet its titanium-alloy construction and quantum processing cores indicated technology centuries beyond current capabilities. The discovery has transformed Billings into an international hub for xenoarchaeology research, with the newly established Institute for Temporal Anomalies drawing scientists and theorists from across the globe to study this enigmatic fusion of biology and advanced robotics that challenges our understanding of both history and technological evolution.
